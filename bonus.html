Auburn Aubie

Aubie is an anthropomorphic tiger in orange and black in color. It is represented for football.
A popular character among Auburn fans and one of the most animated mascots in the country, Aubie is the living spirit of Auburn.

  1. Aubie's existence began as a cartoon character that first appeared on the Auburn/Hardin-Simmons football program cover on October 3, 1959.
  2. Birmingham Post-Herald artist Phil Neel created the cartoon Tiger who continued to adorn Auburn program covers for 18 years.
  3. Aubie's appearances on game programs proved to be somewhat of a good luck charm for Coach Ralph "Shug" Jordan's teams.
